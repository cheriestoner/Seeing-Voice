Understanding Beyond Sound

Beyond Sound offers a multisensory approach to experiencing sound for those who cannot hear. Instead of relying solely on spectrograms, we break sound into its key components and visualize each in unique, intuitive ways.

The Central Experience

The central panel shows a dynamic particle system that responds to sound in real-time. Particles move, change color, and interact based on the sound's characteristics:

  • Pitch affects the vertical position and color of particles
  • Volume affects the size and movement speed
  • Timbre affects the shape and texture
  • Rhythm affects the pulsation patterns

Element Panels

The side panels break down sound into its core elements, with specialized visualizations for each component:

  • Pitch Panel: Shows the "height" of sound from low to high frequencies
  • Intensity Panel: Shows the energy and loudness over time
  • Timbre Panel: Shows the "color" or texture of sound
  • Rhythm Panel: Shows patterns and beats in the sound
  • Speech Panel: Recognizes and transcribes human speech
  • Harmonics Panel: Shows the complex overtones that make up rich sounds

Learning From Patterns

The pattern library at the bottom shows common sound patterns to help you recognize them in the wild. Each pattern has unique visual characteristics that you can learn to associate with specific sounds.
